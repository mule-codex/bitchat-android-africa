# bitchat for Africa (Android)
bitchat is an Android messaging app that works without internet or mobile networks. It uses Bluetooth and long-range tech to connect phones across distances, enabling communication with relatives at home where internet or data bundles are unavailable or too expensive. It bypasses network control, slow internet, and protects user privacy.

Key Points:
1. No mobile network needed; messages travel phone-to-phone via Bluetooth.
2. Works where internet is slow, expensive, or unavailable.
3. No phone number or mobile money needed; no automatic charges.
4. Battery-saving design for power outages and load shedding.
5. Messages hop through nearby phones to reach distant users.
6. Offline message storage until recipients reconnect.
6. Secure, password-protected group chats without central servers.
7. Efficient small packets reduce data and power use.
8. Fake messages sent occasionally to protect privacy.
9. Plans for long-range tech like WiFi Direct and LoRa.
10. Optional secure internet
